  ## LangChain for Multimodal Apps

  LangChain
  is
  a
  framework
  designed
  to
  build
  applications
  that
  integrate
  multiple
  AI
  models, especially those focusing on language understanding, generation, and multimodal capabilities. For multimodal apps, LangChain facilitates seamless interaction between text, image, and even audio models, enabling developers to create complex workflows that can process and analyze different types of data.
